Siem Reap Expansion Survey

Cultural Heritage & Business Hub

Comprehensive location analysis for expansion

5 Days
Duration
5 Locations
Sites Surveyed

Overview

Our comprehensive five-day survey of Siem Reap focused on identifying optimal locations for establishing our regional headquarters in Cambodia's cultural and tourism capital. The expedition covered strategic locations from the central business district to emerging commercial zones, evaluating each site's potential for supporting our expansion goals while considering factors such as infrastructure, accessibility, and local market dynamics.

Specific Locations Surveyed

Siem Reap Central Business District

Prime commercial zone near Pub Street with excellent connectivity and tourist foot traffic

Airport Road Commercial Complex

Modern business facilities with proximity to international airport and major hotels

Wat Bo Village Technology Park

Emerging tech hub with government incentives and modern infrastructure development

Angkor Business Center

Established commercial district with strong local business community and cultural significance

Siem Reap Riverside Development Zone

New waterfront development with premium office spaces and recreational amenities

Trip Highlights

Identified Wat Bo Village Technology Park as the optimal location due to government incentives and modern infrastructure

Established relationships with local government officials and secured preliminary development approvals

Conducted workforce assessment and identified strong local talent pool in technology and tourism sectors

Negotiated competitive lease terms for 5,000 sq ft facility with expansion options

Explored partnership opportunities with Angkor University for internship and recruitment programs

Trip Expenses

Accommodation (5 nights) $750
Transportation & Tours $480
Meals & Business Dinners $650
Site Survey Materials $200
Legal & Consultation Fees $350
Miscellaneous $120
Total $2,550

Conclusion

The Siem Reap expansion survey successfully identified Wat Bo Village Technology Park as our preferred location for establishing operations in Cambodia's cultural capital. The combination of government incentives, modern infrastructure, and access to both local and international talent makes this location ideal for our regional expansion strategy. The relationships established with local stakeholders and the preliminary approvals obtained during this trip have significantly accelerated our timeline for establishing operations. We are now prepared to move forward with lease negotiations and begin the process of setting up our Siem Reap office.
